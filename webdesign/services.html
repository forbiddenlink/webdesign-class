Our Services

We are proud to provide many different services to our customers! We started out only being able to offer dine-in and take-out, but we have expanded to delivery within a half an hour of our establishment, and also now offer catering.


We have just remodeled both the inside and outside of our building, including repaving our parking lot. We take pride in our food, and want our customers to enjoy their surroundings as they dine with us. We have a jukebox, a dartboard, and a pooltable if you feel like dancing or gaming down while you eat, or our restaurant area provides a pleasure dine-in experience. Or, if you enjoy sitting outside to eat during spring and summer seasons, we have recently upgraded our porch and now offer patio dining!


Our catering services can provide full-dine in services for up to 50 people. We have a large selection of catering menu items that you can choose from, depending on your needs. We can also decorate for any occasion for your party for an extra fee.


Not only do we do catering within our own establishment, we can bring our services to you ! With catering out, we provide the food, we serve it, and we clean it all up when finished. If you need us to decorate, we can do that too!
